7 things your mobile attribution tool doesn't do (but should)

What bidding strategies delivered the highest growth. True ROI with IAPs AND ad revenues. And which users saw you on the web, came to mobile, and bought something offline. 

Modern attribution gives you more. And, it adapts to your business rather than demanding you adapt to it.
