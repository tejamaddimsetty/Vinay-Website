Under the Hood of Rovio's SKAdnetwork Setup

ON-DEMAND WEBINAR

What does it take to run SKAdNetwork-based campaigns successfully? While the industry scrambles to adapt to the massive disruption that comes with the release of iOS 14.5, Rovio is leading the way in readiness. And they've shared insights into their setup and results to help you get up to speed.

Watch experts from Rovio, Vungle, and Singular discuss how Rovio tackled iOS 14 readiness and SKAdNetwork testing. The expert panel covers:

  • Critical components of Rovio's SKAdnetwork setup
  • Key findings and learnings Rovio uncovered in end-to-end testing
  • Tips and best practices for optimizing SKAdNetwork campaigns
